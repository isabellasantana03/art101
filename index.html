About Me

Hey! My name is Isabella Santana and I'm majoring in Sociology with a concentration in Global Information & Technology. I was born and raised in San Francisco, and my family still lives there. I have a dog named Canelo and a bird named Bello. I love animals and really want to get a pet of my own soon. In my free time, I enjoy dancing and belong to an Afro Brazillian dance group called Fogo Na Roupa. I also like to tinker with jewelery making and have some experience with metal arts design.
