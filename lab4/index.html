Baking a Cake

  • 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  • 2. Gather Ingredients: a. Flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, eggs, milk, oil, vanilla extract
  • 3. Mix Dry Ingredients: a. Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, and Salt in a large mixing bowl b. Mix thoroughly to blend ingredients together
    • 4. Mix Wet Ingredients: a. In another bowl, crack the eggs b. Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract c. Whisk until the mixture is smooth 5. Combine Wet and Dry ingredients a. Pour the wet mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients b. Mix until the batter is smooth and there are no lumps 6. Prepare Baking Pan: a. Grease the baking pan with non-stick spray 7. Pour batter into Pan: 8. Bake the Cake: a. Place the pan in the preheated oven b. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes 9. Remove Cake from the Oven: a. Let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes
